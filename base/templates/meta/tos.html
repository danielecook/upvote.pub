Rights
  • Your personal data, including your voting and browsing history will not be intentionally shared with third parties.
  • Your comments are public by design and are accessible by third parties. We will never modify the content of comments, but we may remove them.
  • When logged in, you will not be served any third-party content (scripts, cookies, or images).
Responsibilities
  • You may not make automated requests to the site for the purpose of voting or commenting. Other types of automated requests must be limited to once every thirty seconds.
  • Posting links with the primary goal of directing traffic to outside sites, or increasing their search engine visibility, will be construed as spam and may result in account bans.
  • You must not abuse the site by knowingly posting malicious code, or links to malicious sites, that could harm other users. If you find a security or privacy bug, please report it to admin@upvote.pub.
  • You may not use the site to harass other users.
  • You may not use the site to post inappropriate content which includes but is not limited to pornographic content.
  • You may not post the personal information of users outside of their names and affiliations.
  • Accounts consuming an unreasonable proportion of site resources, or posting harassing or innapropriate content are subject to being banned from the site.
